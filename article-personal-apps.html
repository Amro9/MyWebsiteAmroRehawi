When everyone can build their own app

AI changes software from something made for groups into something shaped around individuals.

We are moving into a time where, thanks to AI, everyone can create applications tailored exactly to their own needs. Developing software is no longer something reserved for a specific group of people.

You are no longer just the user of an app, but also its owner. Possibly an app so deeply personalized that, in the end, it is only usable by a single person.

What will become far more important is the integration between apps. How will my app and my data coexist and interact with those of others?

So-called APIs are interfaces that allow one app to communicate with another. But this form of communication is arrogant. It simply says: “Here is my structure, now you do the rest.” No app will be willing to learn the language or structure of another.

Maybe what we will need instead is something in between. Something that listens to both sides, understands them, and finds the best possible way to connect two apps without requiring either one to adapt to the other.
